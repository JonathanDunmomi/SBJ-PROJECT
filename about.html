About Us

Cooking and Food Preparation: This involves the actual cooking and preparation of food items on the restaurant's menu. It includes tasks like chopping ingredients, grilling, frying, baking, and plating dishes. Food Presentation: Ensuring that dishes are visually appealing and served attractively to guests. Table Service: Waitstaff or servers are responsible for taking orders, serving food and beverages, and providing a pleasant dining experience to guests. Customer Service: Greeting and Seating: Welcoming guests as they arrive, helping them find suitable seating, and providing menus. Taking Orders: Recording guests' food and beverage selections accurately. Handling Customer Inquiries and Complaints: Addressing guest questions, concerns, and complaints in a professional and courteous manner. Kitchen Operations: Kitchen Management: Overseeing the functioning of the kitchen, including staff scheduling, inventory management, and ensuring food safety and hygiene. Quality Control: Ensuring that dishes meet the restaurant's quality standards and are consistent in taste and presentation. Timely Preparation: Coordinating the timing of dish preparation to ensure that orders are served promptly.

Mr Segun Akintayo is a dedicated owner of the SBJ Foods and Drinks, A cozy restaurant known for its warm
ambience and homemade comfort food. With over 10years in the industry, he has cultivated a loyal customer
base by focusing on fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and personalised service. His passion for cooking and
commitment to the community are evident in every dish, making his restaurant a favorite spot for both locals and visitors.
Despite the challenges of running a small business, Segun remains hands-on, ensuring that each guest feels at home
from the moment they step through the door.
